Crab Salad Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need for this summer-perfect crab salad recipe:
  • Imitation crab meat: This recipe starts with a pound of imitation crab meat.
  • Celery: Finely chopped celery lends flavor and texture.
  • Dressing and mayonnaise: A combination of mayonnaise and reduced-fat ranch dressing ensures a flavorful, creamy result.
  • Sugar: White sugar adds subtle sweetness.
  • Parsley: Minced fresh parsley gives the crab salad a bright pop of color and flavor.

How to Make a Crab Salad

Here’s a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make this homemade crab salad recipe:
  1. Mix the ingredients until combined.
  2. Chill overnight.
  3. Stir before serving.

Can You Make Crab Salad Ahead of Time?

Yes! In fact, since the crab salad will need to chill overnight, it’s best to make it a day or two in advance. You can store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to four days.

Can You Freeze Crab Salad?

We don’t recommend freezing crab salad. Mayonnaise doesn’t freeze well, as it tends to separate during the thawing process. Fortunately, this recipe is so delicious there likely won’t be any leftovers!
